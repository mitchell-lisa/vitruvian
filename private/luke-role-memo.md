### Why 5%

You floated ~3% as a fair reflection of current bandwidth. I'm proposing 5%
because:

1. You are owning a function I cannot staff myself — engineering operations
   is the critical path to a working prototype.
2. Your Air Force credentials are load-bearing for the military GTM — you are
   both the target user and the access point to AFWERX / SOFWERX.
3. The 18-year planning-and-execution track record is the single biggest
   credibility signal on the team slide.
4. Family-trust premium — we both know what this is worth and why it matters.

5% sits cleanly between a standard advisor grant (0.25–1%) and a full-time
co-founder split (15–25%). It's the "key founding hire, part-time now,
full-time eligible later" band.
