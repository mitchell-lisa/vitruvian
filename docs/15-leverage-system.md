# 15. LEVERAGE SYSTEM

Four types of leverage. You are using none of them.

## The four multipliers

| Type | What it is | Who pays the cost | Who captures the upside |
|------|-------------|--------------------|--------------------------|
| **Capital** | Other people's money | Investors / customers / lenders | You (via equity) |
| **People** | Other people's time | Employees / contractors / partners | You (via operating control) |
| **Technology** | Code that runs while you sleep | Fixed upfront | You (every execution) |
| **Media** | Distribution you own, not rent | Your reputation | You (compounding) |

The wealthiest founders stack all four. You are currently running on zero.

## Stacking — the multiplier effect

One lever = linear. Two = exponential. Four = 100x.

| Stack | What happens |
|-------|---------------|
| Capital + People | Hire VPs to spend the capital you raised |
| Capital + Technology | AI ops means the capital goes further per $ |
| People + Media | Your team's content compounds your reach |
| Technology + Media | One blog post distributed by auto-publishing hits 10 channels |
| All four | $3M raised + VP Sales from Hyperice + AI-ops + quarterly NFL index = you're on ESPN by Q3, at $5M ARR by Q4, closing strategic acquirer conversation by Q8 |
