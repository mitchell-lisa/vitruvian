# 11. WEALTH BLUEPRINT — $100K → $1M / month

Leverage + speed. No side quests.

## Business model — hybrid, recurring, data-backed

| Layer | What | Margin | Role |
|-------|------|--------|------|
| Hardware lease | $2–3k / machine / month | 20–30% | Foot in the door. Recurring. |
| SaaS per athlete | $50 / athlete / month | 80%+ | Scales with roster, not machines |
| Data licensing | Injury-risk models to insurers, leagues, workers' comp | 90%+ | The real business |
| Marketplace | 30% take on third-party protocols (PTs publish) | 95%+ | Platform flywheel |
| API / embed | Hyperice, Therabody, Catapult pay to show Modulor analytics in their tools | 95%+ | Distribution without sales |

## Revenue stack — priority order

1. **Hardware lease** — easiest to close, seeds the data flywheel
2. **Per-athlete SaaS** — immediate recurring, low friction
3. **Data licensing** — highest margin, longest ramp, biggest moat
4. **Marketplace** — network effects, compounds over time
5. **API access** — last, once the data is proven

## Scaling strategy — three phases

### Phase 1 — $0 → $100K/mo (months 0–9)

- 5 paid pilots (lease model, $3k/mo each = $15K/mo from hardware)
- 500 athletes on SaaS @ $50 = $25K/mo
- 1 data-licensing LOI signed (not yet billing)
- $3–5M seed closed from strategics + sports-tech fund
- **Milestone: $40K/mo revenue, $100K/mo signed pipeline**

### Phase 2 — $100K → $500K/mo (months 9–24)

- 30 team / clinic deployments ($90K/mo hardware)
- 5,000 athletes on SaaS ($250K/mo)
- First data license billing ($50–100K/mo)
- Marketplace live — 10 PT authors, $20K/mo take
- Strategic partnership with Hyperice **or** Therabody (channel)
- **Milestone: $500K/mo, 70% recurring**

### Phase 3 — $500K → $1M/mo (months 24–36)

- 100+ institutional customers (pro + college + elite PT clinics)
- 20,000+ athletes on SaaS ($1M/mo)
- 3–5 data licensees ($500K/mo combined)
- Marketplace 100+ authors
- Acquisition conversations open — Hyperice, Therabody, Nike, WHOOP
- **Milestone: $1.5M/mo, acquisition offers at $150M+**

## Leverage moves (do all of these)

- **Leasing bakes capex into customer's opex** — 5x close rate, no balance-sheet drag
- **Every session = data = appreciating asset** — revenue today, moat tomorrow
- **Strategic partner owns distribution** — you don't hire 30 reps
- **API turns competitors into distribution** — Hyperice pays you to be inside their product
- **Athlete equity buys marketing you can't afford** — LeBron / Mahomes = free top-of-funnel
- **PTs build the content library** — marketplace, not in-house

## Speed moves (do all of these)

- **Institutional only until $1M/mo** — no B2C, no DTC, no merch
- **One market deep before breadth** — sports first; rehab/PT/geriatric later
- **Hire VP Sales from Hyperice or Catapult month 3** — they bring the rolodex
- **Buy the leasing partner on day one** — don't build financing in-house
- **Outside counsel for data licensing** — don't DIY IP law
- **Kill every $5K idea** — every hour on something small is an hour not on something big

## Anti-patterns — do not touch

- Selling machines outright
- Building a mobile consumer app
- Opening a flagship studio / clinic
- Courses, certifications as a standalone product
- Giving away data rights in exchange for a pilot discount
- Raising from generalist VCs with no sports or medtech thesis
- Any feature, build, or deal that doesn't move the $500M number
